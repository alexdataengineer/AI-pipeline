## The Challenge

HR and recruitment teams spend significant time manually searching through candidate CVs and resumes to find qualified candidates. This manual process is:
- **Time-consuming:** 2-3 hours per day per recruiter
- **Error-prone:** Human oversight leads to missed opportunities
- **Not scalable:** Cannot efficiently handle large candidate pools
- **Costly:** High time-to-fill and external recruitment fees

---

## The Solution

An enterprise-grade RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline that transforms unstructured CV data into an intelligent, queryable knowledge base using cutting-edge AI technology.

### How It Works

1. **Automated Ingestion:** CVs are automatically processed and indexed daily
2. **AI-Powered Search:** Natural language queries return instant, accurate results
3. **Context-Aware Responses:** AI understands questions and provides relevant answers with source citations
4. **Scalable Architecture:** Handles thousands of documents with sub-2-second response times
